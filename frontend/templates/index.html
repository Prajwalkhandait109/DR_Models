    About this project

    RetinaScan is a final-year B.Tech research prototype that compares CNN and Transformer architectures on diabetic retinopathy severity grading. We trained four models on the APTOS-2019 and EyePACS datasets and surface Grad-CAM explanations so clinicians can independently verify each prediction.
